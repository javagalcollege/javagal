About Us

Government First Grade College Javagal was established in the year 1991. It is situated in Javagal village, Arasikere Taluk, Hassan district in the southern state of Karnataka. The Lakshminarasimha temple at Javagal is an example of mid-13th century Hoysala architecture. It is located about 12km from Halebidu, capital of the Hoysala Empire. The college is connected by National Highway 73 which is convenient to access for students. The college has been promoting the cause of value-based higher education to rural students since 1991. The college is permanently affiliated to the University of Mysore. The college has got the recognition of 2(f) & 12(B) of the UGC act, 1956 on 15th March 2016. The college has also adapted the University’s Choice Based Credit System (CBCS) scheme in the year 2018 and NEP in 2021 onwards. The college offers undergraduate courses in BA & Bcom programmes.
